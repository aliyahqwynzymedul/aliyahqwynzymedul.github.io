HulaHallyu? HallyuHula? What is this game all about?

The Hallyu Wave has swept the world, and there is no denying that K-Drama and K-Pop continue to serve as comforters when the rest of the world is riding high on chaos. From sweet nothings in K-Dramas to head banging beat drops in K-Pop, we love it all!

So, if you are an avid Hallyu fan, then this game is for you!

HulaHallyu is a quiz-type game that will test your expertise about the Korean pop culture.

Mechanics: How to Play HulaHallyu?

1. Before you start the game, you will be asked to choose a category you want to play. The categories are K-Pop and K-Drama. There are three rounds per category: easy, average, and difficult. Each round has three questions.

2. Once you start the game, you will be presented a question about your chosen category that you will have to guess.

3. You are provided with a set of options to choose from and all you have to do is to click the option you think is correct.

4.Each correct answer in the easy, average, and difficult rounds will give you 1, 3, and 5 points, respectively.
